Chapter 1

The Village of Shadows

Deep inside a thick forest stood a quiet village called Shadowvale. During the day it looked peaceful. Children played near the well and farmers worked in their fields.

But when the sun went down, the village became silent.

No one walked outside at night.

Because something terrible lived in the darkness.

Chapter 2

The Terrifying Legend

Long ago, a tired traveler came to the village asking for food and shelter.

But the villagers feared strangers.

They shouted at him and chased him away.

Before disappearing into the forest, the traveler turned around and said in a cold voice:

"You refused kindness... now your village will know fear forever."

That night, the curse began.

Chapter 3

The Midnight Footsteps

Every night at exactly 12:00, strange footsteps echoed through the village.

Tap... Tap... Tap...

People heard them outside their doors.

But when they opened the door...

No one was there.

Chapter 4

The New Family

Many years later, a boy named Kabir moved to Shadowvale with his parents.

Kabir was brave and curious.

When he heard the villagers talking about ghosts and curses, he didn't believe them.

"Ghosts are not real," Kabir laughed.

But the villagers looked worried.

...
Chapter 5

The Warning

An old woman stopped Kabir near the well.

Her voice trembled.

"Never go outside after midnight," she warned.

Kabir only smiled.

He wanted to know the truth.

...
Chapter 6

Midnight

That night Kabir stayed awake.

When the clock struck twelve, he quietly stepped outside.

The village was covered in thick fog.

Then he heard it.

Tap... Tap... Tap...

Footsteps behind him.

Kabir slowly turned around.

A tall shadow stood in the darkness.
...
Chapter 7

The Shadow Man

The figure looked like a man, but his face was hidden.

Kabir gathered his courage.

"Who are you?" he asked.

The shadow spoke in a deep whisper.

"I am the traveler your village rejected."

"My curse has lived here for many years."

...
Chapter 8

The Secret

Kabir felt sorry for the shadow.

"I'm sorry for what the villagers did," Kabir said.

"You can stay in our house."

The shadow became silent.

Then he slowly laughed.

A cold, terrifying laugh.
...
Chapter 9

The Real Curse

The shadow stepped closer.

"You still don't understand," it said.

"I was never the cursed one."

Kabir felt the ground shake.

The shadow's voice became darker.

"The curse needed a new soul to survive."

...
Chapter 10

The Shocking Ending

Suddenly the shadow disappeared.

The fog lifted.

The village became quiet again.

The curse seemed gone.

But the next night...

The villagers heard footsteps again.

Tap... Tap... Tap...

They opened their doors and saw a tall shadow walking through the village.

This time...

The shadow looked like a boy.

And its glowing eyes looked exactly like Kabir's.

The curse had chosen its new owner.

The End

A Cursed Village...
A Terrifying Secret

Deep in a dark forest lies a village called Shadowvale, cursed by a terrifying legend. A brave boy named Kabir tries to uncover the truth behind the eerie midnight footsteps and discovers a shocking secret that changes everything.

But in a place where curses are never easily broken, one terrifying question remains:

Who will be the curse's next owner?
